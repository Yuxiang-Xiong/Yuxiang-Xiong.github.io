Research

Working Papers

Exposure to Chinese Imports and Biodiversity: Evidence from U.S. Biodiversity Survey over 1990-2007

Zheng Yu, Yuxiang Xiong, Xuechun Wang

This project studies whether biodiversity changed after the transfer of U.S. manufacturing following China's entry into the WTO. The analysis uses North American bird survey data and ArcGIS-based biodiversity indicators.

Empirical Study of the Finance Benefit Policy Impact on Agriculture Development: Evidence from China

Quan Xiao, Yuxiang Xiong

This paper treats farmer subsidies as a quasi-natural experiment and studies changes in agricultural production before and after the policy. Outcomes include total factor productivity, green total factor productivity, and crop area.

No One Lends a Helping Hand in the Flood: Wealth Transfer and Unemployment in the United States

Yuxiang Xiong

This project studies the externality of wealth transfers after floods, using distance between counties, state capitals, and Washington, DC as instrumental variation.
